A Referral Note communicates pertinent information from a provider who is requesting services of another provider of clinical or non-clinical services. The information in this document includes the reason for the referral and additional information that would augment decision making and care delivery.

Examples of referral situations are: * When a patient is referred from a family physician to a cardiologist for cardiac evaluation. * When patient is sent by a cardiologist to an emergency department for angina. * When a patient is referred by a nurse practitioner to an audiologist for hearing screening. * When a patient is referred by a hospitalist to social services.
